MEEFMilyon - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Information We Collect

MEEFMilyon collects the following information: email address, display name, and game statistics. This information is used to provide the game experience, leaderboards, and user profiles.

Authentication

We use Firebase Authentication for user login. You can sign in with email/password or Google Sign-In. We do not store your password directly.

Game Data

We store your game progress, scores, and BOOM points in Firebase Firestore. This data is used to provide leaderboards and track your achievements.

Advertising

MEEFMilyon displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device information and advertising identifiers to serve relevant ads. Please refer to Google privacy policy for more details.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except as required by law or as described in this policy.

Data Security

We use Firebase security rules and encryption to protect your data.

Children Privacy

MEEFMilyon is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, please contact us at: info@meeftoken.com
